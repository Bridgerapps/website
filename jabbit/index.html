Everything you need to manage your protocol

Powerful features designed by people who understand the complexity of peptide tracking.

Smart Injection Tracking

Log every injection with 14-zone site rotation. Get smart suggestions for your next site based on your history and optimal rotation patterns.

Vial Management & Calculator

Track multiple vials with built-in reconstitution calculator. Enter peptide amount and diluent volume to get exact mcg/unit concentrations instantly.

Flexible Dosing Schedules

Choose from daily, every other day, 2x/week, 3x/week, weekly, 5-on-2-off, or fully custom schedules. Set preferred times and get reminders.

Titration & Escalation

Plan multi-phase dose escalation with automatic phase advancement. Perfect for protocols that start low and gradually increase.

Cycle Planning

Configure weeks on and weeks off for cycling protocols. The app tracks where you are in your cycle and adjusts your schedule accordingly.

Real-Time Serum Level Charts

Visualize estimated serum levels based on pharmacokinetic data. View day, week, month, or 90-day trends with smooth PK curve visualization.

Progress Tracking

Log weight, body measurements, and progress photos. Sync with Apple Health for automatic weight tracking. See your journey over time.

Peptide Encyclopedia

Built-in reference library with pharmacokinetic data including half-life, time to peak, and dosing guidelines for common peptides.

Private iCloud Sync

Your data syncs automatically across all your devices via your personal iCloud account. We never see or store your information.
