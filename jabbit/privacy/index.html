Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 17, 2024

The Short Version

Your data is yours. Jabbit stores all your information locally on your device and syncs via your personal iCloud account. We never collect, store, or have access to your health data on our servers.

1. Introduction

Bridger Apps ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Jabbit mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Jabbit was designed from the ground up with a privacy-first architecture, meaning your personal health data never touches our servers.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Jabbit does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following on our servers:

  • Injection logs and history
  • Peptide information and dosing data
  • Vial inventory and reconstitution details
  • Progress photos, weight, or measurements
  • Health-related notes or observations
  • Personal identification information

3. How Your Data Is Stored

All data you enter into Jabbit is stored in two places:

  • Locally on your device: Your data is stored on your iPhone or iPad using Apple's secure on-device storage.
  • Your personal iCloud account: If you have iCloud enabled, your data syncs to your personal iCloud account to enable access across your devices. This sync is handled entirely by Apple's CloudKit framework and is encrypted end-to-end.

We do not have access to your iCloud data. Only you can access your data through your Apple ID credentials.

4. Information We May Collect

While we do not collect your health data, we may collect limited information for app functionality:

  • Crash reports: If you opt in to share crash data with developers through iOS settings, we may receive anonymized crash reports to help improve app stability.
  • App Store analytics: Apple may provide us with aggregated, anonymized analytics about app usage (such as number of downloads) through App Store Connect.
  • Purchase information: If you make in-app purchases, the transaction is processed by Apple. We receive confirmation of the purchase but not your payment details.

5. Third-Party Services

Jabbit uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple iCloud (CloudKit): For syncing your data across devices. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat: For managing subscriptions. RevenueCat receives anonymized purchase data but not your health information. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

6. Apple HealthKit

Jabbit may optionally integrate with Apple HealthKit to read or write weight data. This integration:

  • Requires your explicit permission
  • Only accesses data types you specifically authorize
  • Never transmits HealthKit data to our servers or any third party
  • Is used solely to display or sync weight information within the app

7. Data Security

Your data is protected by:

  • Apple's device-level encryption
  • iCloud's end-to-end encryption for synced data
  • Your device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID

Since we don't store your data on our servers, there is no central database that could be compromised.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains on your device and iCloud account until you choose to delete it. You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Using the "Delete All Data" option in the app settings
  • Deleting the app from your device
  • Removing Jabbit data from your iCloud storage

9. Children's Privacy

Jabbit is not intended for use by children under 17 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
