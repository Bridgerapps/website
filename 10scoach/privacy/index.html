Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

10sCoach ("the App") is developed by Bridger Apps. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

What We Collect

Data Purpose Linked to You?
Name Display in coach/player profiles Yes
Email address Account identification via Sign in with Apple Yes
User ID Account management and data sync Yes
Photos and videos Lesson media, demo videos, video feedback Yes
Coaching content Lesson notes, messages, drill assignments, goals, match/practice logs Yes
Usage data App interaction for improving functionality No
Purchase history Subscription status management Yes

How We Store Your Data

All data is stored in Apple's iCloud (CloudKit) under your personal Apple ID. We do not operate independent servers. Your data syncs across your devices via iCloud and is subject to Apple's iCloud privacy practices.

Coach-to-player data sharing uses Apple's CloudKit sharing (CKShare). Private coach notes are never shared.

Sign in with Apple

We use Sign in with Apple for authentication. You may choose to share or hide your email address. We receive only the information you authorize Apple to provide.

Third-Party Services

We do not share your data with third parties. We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Tracking

We do not track you across other apps or websites.

Children's Privacy

10sCoach supports junior player accounts managed by a parent. Parent accounts control all data for linked junior players. We do not knowingly collect data directly from children under 13 without parental involvement.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data from within the App under Settings. This removes your data from CloudKit.

Data Security

Your data is protected by multiple layers of security:

• Apple's device-level encryption — All data on your device is encrypted
• iCloud's end-to-end encryption — Synced data is encrypted in transit and at rest
• Your device security — Protected by your passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID
• CKShare permissions — Players have read-only access to coach-created content
• No central database — Since we don't store your data on our servers, there is no central database that could be breached

Your Rights Under GDPR (European Union and EEA Users)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Because we do not store your coaching data on our servers, most of these rights are automatically satisfied—your data remains in your exclusive control.

Your rights include:

• Right of Access (Article 15) — Since your data is stored on your device and iCloud, you already have direct access.

• Right to Rectification (Article 16) — You can correct any inaccurate data directly in the App.

• Right to Erasure (Article 17) — You can delete your data at any time using the App's settings or by deleting the App.

• Right to Data Portability (Article 20) — Your data is stored in your iCloud account under your control.

• Right to Restriction of Processing (Article 18) — You can disable iCloud sync or delete specific data within the App.

• Right to Withdraw Consent — You may withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

• Right to Lodge a Complaint — You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority in your EU/EEA member state.

Legal Basis for Processing: For the limited technical data we may receive (crash reports, purchase confirmations), our legal basis is:

• Contract performance (Article 6(1)(b)) — To provide the App and subscription services
• Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f)) — To improve app stability and prevent fraud
• Consent (Article 6(1)(a)) — Where you have opted in to share crash data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@bridgerapps.com. We will respond within one (1) month as required by GDPR.

International Data Transfers

Bridger Apps is based in the United States. If you access the App from outside the United States, please be aware:

• Your coaching data is stored on your local device and in your iCloud account. Apple's iCloud has data centers worldwide and processes data according to Apple's Privacy Policy and applicable data transfer mechanisms.

• Limited technical data (crash reports, purchase confirmations) may be processed in the United States.

Transfer Mechanisms (EU/UK): For any personal data transferred from the EU/EEA or UK to the United States, we rely on:

• The EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework (where applicable)
• Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission
• Your explicit consent where required

California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

• Right to Know — You can request information about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected.

• Right to Delete — You can request deletion of your personal information.

• Right to Correct — You can request correction of inaccurate personal information.

• Right to Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing — We do not sell or share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising, so this right does not apply.

• Right to Non-Discrimination — We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

To exercise your California privacy rights, contact us at privacy@bridgerapps.com. We will verify your identity before processing your request.
