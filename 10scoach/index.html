Powerful features designed by people who understand the coaching relationship.

Quick Lesson Logging

Log lessons in under two minutes. Rate skills across 11 tennis categories, add notes, attach photos and video, and set focus areas for next time.

Player Progress Tracking

Visualize skill development with trend charts, radar diagrams, and activity heatmaps. Track NTRP, UTR, or WTN ratings over time.

Drill Library

Choose from 50+ built-in drills organized by skill category, or create your own. Assign drills to players and track completion between lessons.

Session Scheduling

Schedule one-off or recurring weekly lessons. Set reminders, manage cancellations, and optionally sync with your device calendar.

In-App Messaging

Communicate directly with players and parents. Send text, photos, and video clips with per-message visibility controls for junior players.

Demo Video Library

Build a personal library of instructional videos. Record once, share with individual players or broadcast to your entire roster with timestamped feedback.

Match & Practice Logging

Players log practice sessions and match results with set-by-set scores. Coaches see everything before the next lesson for a complete picture.

Goal Setting

Set development goals for each player with target dates and related skills. Track progress together and celebrate achievements along the way.

Private iCloud Sync

Your data syncs automatically across all your devices via your personal iCloud account. Coaching data is shared securely between connected coaches and players.

Your privacy is not negotiable

We don't run servers. We don't collect analytics. We don't track you. 10sCoach stores everything in your personal iCloud account and uses Apple's CloudKit sharing to connect coaches and players directly.

That means your lesson history, player development data, and private coaching notes stay between the people they belong to. There's no Bridger Apps database in the middle—by design.
